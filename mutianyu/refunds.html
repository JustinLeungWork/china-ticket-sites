Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We want your visit to the Mutianyu Great Wall to go smoothly. Here's exactly when you can get a refund.

SituationRefund
Cancelled 48+ hours before your visit date100% full refund
Attraction closes or tickets unavailable on your date100% full refund
Cancelled within 48 hours of visit dateNo refund

How to Request a Refund or Date Change

Email hello@mutianyu-tickets.com with your booking email address and the visit date. We'll process refunds within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

If We Can't Fulfil Your Booking

On rare occasions, tickets may sell out on your requested date or the attraction may close unexpectedly. In these cases, we will contact you immediately and issue a full refund — no questions asked.
