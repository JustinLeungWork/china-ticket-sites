Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Mutianyu Great Wall tickets are date-specific and non-transferable under Chinese regulations. Please book carefully.

SituationRefund
Cancelled 48+ hours before your visit date100% full refund
Wall closed or tickets unavailable on your date100% full refund
Cancelled within 48 hours of visit dateNo refund
No-showNo refund
Date change requested 72+ hours before visitFree date change (subject to availability)
Date change within 72 hoursNot guaranteed — contact us

How to Request a Refund or Date Change

Email support@mutianyu-tickets.com with your booking email address and visit date. We'll process refunds within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

Advance Booking Requirement

We require at least 2 days' notice to process your booking on the official Chinese system. Same-day and next-day bookings aren't possible. We recommend booking 3–5 days ahead, especially during peak season (May holidays, October Golden Week, summer weekends).

If We Can't Fulfil Your Booking

If tickets are unavailable or the wall closes unexpectedly, we'll contact you immediately and issue a full refund.
