2. What the Service Does

When you complete a booking and payment, we:

  1. Receive your booking details and passport information.
  2. Purchase the requested ticket(s) on the official system (mutianyu.com) using our authorised accounts.
  3. Forward the QR code(s) to your email address, typically within 24 hours of confirmed payment.

One ticket per passport per day. China's ticketing system enforces this strictly — a passport number can only be used once per calendar day. We do not control this policy. If your group includes multiple visitors, each needs a separate ticket registered to their individual passport.

We require at least 2 days' notice before your visit date. The official Chinese ticketing system requires advance reservation and our procurement process takes time. Same-day or next-day requests cannot be fulfilled.
