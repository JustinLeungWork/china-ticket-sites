Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Governed by Singapore PDPA

MutianyuTickets.com ("we", "us") operates as an independent ticket procurement service. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. What We Collect

To purchase official Chinese attraction tickets on your behalf, we collect:

  • Email address (booking confirmation and QR code delivery)
  • Full name, passport number, nationality, and date of birth for each visitor (required by the Chinese real-name ticketing system)
  • Visit date and ticket type

We do not collect payment card details. All payments are processed by Stripe, Inc., which is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified.

2. Why We Need Passport Details

The official Chinese attraction ticketing system requires real-name registration for every ticket. Each ticket is linked to a specific passport number and the name must match exactly at the gate. We cannot purchase tickets on your behalf without this information.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Passport details are stored in Stripe's secure payment metadata, which is encrypted at rest and in transit. This data is transmitted to our operator via email when your payment is confirmed.

We do not maintain our own database of passport information.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Under our PDPA commitment, passport details are deleted from our operator's email inbox and Stripe metadata after your ticket QR code has been issued — typically within 24 hours of purchase. Email confirmation records (without passport details) are retained for up to 12 months for dispute resolution.

5. Third Parties

  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing and metadata storage. See stripe.com/privacy.
  • Resend, Inc. — transactional email delivery. See resend.com/privacy.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

6. Your Rights (Singapore PDPA)

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at hello@mutianyu-tickets.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

7. Contact

For privacy questions: hello@mutianyu-tickets.com
