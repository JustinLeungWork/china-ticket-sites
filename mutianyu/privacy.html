Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Governed by Singapore PDPA 2012

MutianyuTickets.com is an independent ticket procurement service operated from Singapore. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data, and your rights under the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

MutianyuTickets.com ("we", "us", "our") is the data controller for personal data collected through this website. We are responsible for how your data is handled and can be contacted at hello@mutianyu-tickets.com.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following bases under the PDPA:

  • Contractual necessity: processing your booking requires your name, passport, and email.
  • Explicit consent: you tick a consent checkbox before submitting your passport details, confirming you understand how your data will be used. You may withdraw this consent at any time (see Section 8).
  • Legitimate interest: retaining transaction records for dispute resolution.

4. Why We Need Passport Details

China's official attraction ticketing system mandates real-name registration. Every ticket is registered to a specific passport number, and the name must match exactly at the entrance gate. This is a legal requirement of the Chinese system — not a policy choice of ours. We have no alternative way to purchase tickets on your behalf.

5. How Your Data Is Stored and Transmitted

Passport details are stored within Stripe's secure payment metadata, which is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+). When payment is confirmed, these details are transmitted to our operator via encrypted email (Resend, Inc.) to action the ticket purchase. We do not store passport data in our own database.

No consent banner is shown because we do not use non-essential cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and add a consent mechanism.

9. Your Rights Under the Singapore PDPA

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right of correction: request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Right of withdrawal: withdraw your consent to data processing at any time. Note that withdrawing consent after a booking is placed may mean we cannot fulfil your ticket order (and a refund will be issued).
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal retention obligations.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@mutianyu-tickets.com with the subject line "Data Request". We will respond within 10 business days.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in significant harm to you, we will notify the Singapore Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) within 3 calendar days of discovery, and notify you directly as soon as practicable, in accordance with the PDPA mandatory breach notification obligation.

11. Children's Privacy

This service is intended for use by adults (18+) booking on behalf of themselves or their children. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact and Complaints

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights: hello@mutianyu-tickets.com

If you are unsatisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Commission at pdpc.gov.sg.
