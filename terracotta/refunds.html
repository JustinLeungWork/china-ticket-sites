Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Terracotta Warriors tickets are date-specific and non-transferable under Chinese regulations. Please book carefully.

SituationRefund
Cancelled 48+ hours before your visit date100% full refund
Museum closed or tickets unavailable on your date100% full refund
Cancelled within 48 hours of visit dateNo refund
No-showNo refund
Date change requested 72+ hours before visitFree date change (subject to availability)
Date change within 72 hoursNot guaranteed — contact us

How to Request a Refund or Date Change

Email support@terracotta-tickets.com with your booking email address and visit date. We'll process refunds within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

Booking More Than 7 Days Ahead

We recommend booking at least 7 days before your visit to allow time to secure tickets on the official Chinese system, which often requires advance reservation. Last-minute requests (within 48 hours) may not be possible — please plan ahead.

If We Can't Fulfil Your Booking

If tickets are sold out or the museum closes unexpectedly, we'll contact you immediately and issue a full refund.
