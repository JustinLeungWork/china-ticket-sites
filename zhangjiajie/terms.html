2. What the Service Does

When you complete a booking and payment, we:

  1. Receive your booking details and passport information.
  2. Purchase the requested ticket(s) on the official Wulingyuan system using our authorised accounts.
  3. Forward the QR code(s) to your email address, typically within 24 hours of confirmed payment.

One ticket per passport. Each ticket is registered to a specific passport number. The name on the ticket must match the passport exactly on each entry day. We do not control this policy — it is a legal requirement of China's real-name ticketing system.

Children under 14 enter free with their passport at the gate. We do not sell tickets for children under 14 — they are not bookable through this service and no booking is required for them.

3. Ticket Validity

Your 4-day park pass is valid for 4 consecutive days beginning your chosen first entry date. If you select 1 July as your start date, your ticket is valid 1–4 July inclusive. The validity window is set by the park and cannot be changed after booking.

4. Advance Booking Requirement

We require bookings to be placed at least 2 days ahead of the first entry date (calculated in Beijing time, GMT+8). Same-day or next-day requests cannot be guaranteed. We recommend booking at least 5–7 days in advance, especially during Chinese national holidays and peak travel seasons.
