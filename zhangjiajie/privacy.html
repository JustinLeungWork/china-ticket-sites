Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Governed by Singapore PDPA 2012

Zhangjiajie Forest Park Tickets is an independent ticket procurement service operated from Singapore. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data, and your rights under the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (PDPA).

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

Zhangjiajie Forest Park Tickets ("we", "us", "our") is the data controller for personal data collected through this website. We are an independent ticket procurement service operated from Singapore.

Our Data Protection Officer (DPO) can be reached at support@zhangjiajie-tickets.com for any privacy question, access or correction request, or complaint.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contractual necessity: processing your booking requires your name, passport, and email.
  • Explicit consent: you tick a consent checkbox before submitting passport details, confirming you understand how your data will be used. You may withdraw this consent at any time (see Section 9).
  • Legitimate interest: retaining transaction records for dispute resolution.

4. Why We Need Passport Details

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park (Wulingyuan Scenic Area) uses China's real-name ticketing system, which legally requires every visitor's full name and passport number. One ticket is registered per passport. The name on the ticket must match the passport exactly at the entrance gate on each of the 4 days. This is a legal requirement of the Chinese system we cannot work around.

5. How Your Data Is Stored and Transmitted

Your passport details are held in our own secure booking database (hosted by Neon, encrypted at rest, TLS in transit) and are deliberately not sent to our payment processor — Stripe only receives your payment, email, and a booking reference. After payment, your details are transmitted via Resend, Inc. to our operator, who purchases your ticket. To issue the ticket, your full name and passport number are then entered into the park's official Chinese real-name booking system — this transfer is necessary to fulfil your booking and is covered by the consent you give at checkout (see Section 8). Your passport data is deleted automatically after your 4-day validity window ends (see Section 6).

6. Data Retention

Singapore's PDPA does not fix a maximum retention period; we keep personal data only as long as it is needed for your booking and for related legal or business purposes, and then delete it.

7. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. The only cookies that may be set are essential Stripe session cookies during the checkout process (fraud prevention, governed by Stripe's cookie policy). We do not show a cookie banner as we use no non-essential cookies.

9. Your Rights Under the Singapore PDPA

  • Right of access: request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Right of correction: request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Right of withdrawal: withdraw consent at any time. Withdrawing after booking placement may prevent fulfilment — a full refund will be issued in that case.
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.

Email support@zhangjiajie-tickets.com with subject "Data Request". We respond within 10 business days.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a breach likely to cause significant harm, we will notify the Singapore PDPC within 3 calendar days of discovery and notify you directly as soon as practicable, per the PDPA mandatory breach notification obligation.

11. Children's Privacy

This service is for adults (18+) booking on behalf of themselves or accompanying visitors. Children under 14 enter Zhangjiajie National Forest Park for free and are not booked through our service — we do not collect their data. Youth aged 14–17 have their details collected only for the purpose of purchasing their discounted ticket. Contact us if you believe a child's data has been collected in error and we will delete it promptly.

We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance.
