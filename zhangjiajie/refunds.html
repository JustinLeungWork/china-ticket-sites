Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your Zhangjiajie park pass is valid for 4 consecutive days beginning your first entry date. The ticket is non-refundable once you have first entered the park, as it is a used ticket at that point. Please plan your visit carefully.

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park tickets are date-specific and tied to individual passports. Please book carefully and verify all passport details before payment.

How to Request a Refund or Date Change

Email support@zhangjiajie-tickets.com with your booking reference, email address, and first entry date. We'll process refunds within 5–10 business days to the original payment method.

Important Notes on the 4-Day Validity Window

Your ticket is valid for 4 consecutive days starting from the first entry date you chose at checkout. The refund policy applies to the first entry date — the date you first enter the park. After you have first scanned your QR code at the gate, the ticket is considered used and no refund is possible regardless of how many of the remaining days you use.

If you enter on day 1 and then cannot continue on days 2–3, no partial refund is available — the full 4-day validity period is sold as a single ticket.

If We Can't Fulfil Your Booking

If tickets are sold out or the park closes unexpectedly on your first entry date, we'll contact you immediately and issue a full refund. We'll also check surrounding available dates at no penalty.

Booking More Than 2 Days Ahead

We require bookings to be placed at least 2 days (Beijing time) before the first entry date. During Chinese national holidays (especially Golden Week in October and Spring Festival in January/February), we recommend booking 10–14 days ahead — the park reaches capacity quickly and availability can disappear overnight.
